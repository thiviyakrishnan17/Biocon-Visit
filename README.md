# Biocon Industry Visit — Industry Exposure & R&D Insights

We recently had the opportunity to visit Biocon, and it was a meaningful and insightful experience. The visit gave us exposure to the industry environment and helped us understand how different departments and processes work together in a professional setting.

## Visit highlights
- Guided tour of the facility and an overview of day-to-day operations.
- Introduction to the research and development (R&D) environment, including how innovation, research, and technology contribute to product development.
- Demonstrations of lab safety and quality systems in a commercial setting.
- Opportunities to interact with professionals and ask questions about career paths and workflows.

## What I learned
We were introduced to the research and development (R&D) aspect, where we gained a brief understanding of how innovation, research, and technology contribute to the company’s growth and product development. Overall, the visit broadened my perspective on industry practices and the importance of continuous innovation.
